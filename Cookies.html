Cookie Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

Welcome to PerkSnaps, a Philippines-based platform providing reward codes, featured content, and information for Roblox games worldwide. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at https://www.perksnaps.com (the “Site”).

By accessing or using the Site, you consent to the use of cookies and tracking technologies as described in this policy, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines and applicable international data protection laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA). If you do not agree with this policy, you may adjust your browser settings to limit or disable cookies, though this may affect the Site’s functionality.

This policy is part of our broader Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. For questions, please contact us at contact@perksnaps.com.

1. What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

1.1 Definition of Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They store information that helps the site function properly, remember your preferences, and provide insights to improve user experience.

1.2 Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies, including:

  • Web Beacons/Pixel Tags: Tiny images embedded in web pages or emails to track user interactions, such as page views or email opens.
  • Local Storage: HTML5 local storage or similar mechanisms to store data on your device for site functionality or preferences.
  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers associated with your device or browser to recognize returning users.

1.3 Purpose of Cookies

Cookies and tracking technologies enable us to:

  • Ensure the Site operates correctly and securely.
  • Remember your settings and preferences for a personalized experience.
  • Analyze site usage to improve performance, content, and user satisfaction.
  • Support embedded third-party content, such as videos or social media widgets.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function and cannot be disabled without impairing core features. They enable:

  • Navigation between pages.
  • Access to secure areas of the Site, if applicable.
  • Basic performance and security functions, such as session management.

Legal Basis: These cookies are used based on our legitimate interest in providing a functional website, as permitted under Philippine law and GDPR (where applicable).

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymized data on how visitors interact with the Site, such as:

  • Which pages are visited most frequently.
  • How long users spend on the Site.
  • Error messages or performance issues encountered.

We use this data to optimize the Site’s usability, content, and performance. Examples include cookies set by Google Analytics (see Section 4).

Legal Basis: We obtain your consent for these cookies, as required by the Data Privacy Act and GDPR, through our cookie consent mechanism (if implemented) or your continued use of the Site after notification.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to enhance your experience, such as:

  • Language or region settings.
  • Display preferences (e.g., dark or light theme).
  • Previously viewed content to avoid repetition.

Legal Basis: We obtain your consent for these cookies, as noted above.

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

We may embed content hosted by third-party platforms, such as YouTube videos, Roblox game trailers, or social media widgets (e.g., X share buttons). These third parties may set cookies to:

  • Track your interaction with their content.
  • Personalize their services or advertisements.
  • Collect analytics on embedded content usage.

Legal Basis: Consent is obtained for these cookies, and we provide links to third-party policies (see Section 4).

2.5 Cookie Duration

Cookies may be:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period (e.g., days, months, or years) or until deleted.

3. Managing Cookies

3.1 Browser Settings

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View all cookies stored on your device.
  • Delete specific or all cookies.
  • Block all cookies or specific types (e.g., third-party cookies).
  • Set preferences for cookie acceptance (e.g., prompt for consent).

For guidance, visit your browser’s help section or sites like www.allaboutcookies.org. Common browsers include:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

3.2 Cookie Consent Mechanism

If implemented, we may use a cookie consent banner or pop-up to obtain your explicit consent for non-essential cookies (e.g., performance, functionality, third-party). You can accept, reject, or customize your preferences through this tool. Consent is stored for future visits but can be withdrawn by clearing cookies or contacting us.

3.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect the Site’s functionality, such as:

  • Inability to save preferences (e.g., theme or language).
  • Limited access to certain features or content.
  • Reduced performance or personalized experience.

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are critical to the Site’s operation.

3.4 Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer “Do Not Track” (DNT) settings to signal your preference against tracking. Currently, our Site does not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your cookie preferences as set through browser settings or our consent mechanism.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Analytics Services

We use third-party analytics tools to understand Site usage and improve our services, including:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymized data on user behavior, such as page views, session duration, and referral sources. Google may set cookies (e.g., _ga, _gid) to track these metrics.

For details, review Google’s Privacy Policy and Safeguarding Your Data. To opt out, use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

4.2 Embedded Content

We embed third-party content to enhance the Site, such as:

  • YouTube Videos: Game trailers or promotional videos hosted on YouTube may set cookies to track views, preferences, or ads. See YouTube’s Cookie Policy.
  • Social Media Widgets: Share buttons or feeds from platforms like X may set cookies to track interactions. Review X’s Privacy Policy.

We do not control third-party cookies or their data collection practices. These services are governed by their own policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4.3 Data Sharing

Third-party services may receive anonymized or pseudonymized data via cookies, such as IP addresses or device identifiers, as outlined in their policies. We ensure that any data sharing complies with the Data Privacy Act and GDPR, using safeguards like data processing agreements where required.

5. Data Protection and Your Rights

5.1 Data Privacy Compliance

We process personal data collected via cookies in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and, for EEA users, the GDPR. Our Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your data.

5.2 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding data collected via cookies:

  • Access: Request details of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data, including cookies, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests (e.g., analytics cookies).
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for non-essential cookies at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@perksnaps.com. We will respond within 15 business days, as required by Philippine law, or 30 days for GDPR requests.

5.3 International Data Transfers

As a Philippines-based platform with global users, we may transfer data collected via cookies to third-party providers (e.g., Google, YouTube) located outside the Philippines, including the United States. We ensure such transfers comply with the Data Privacy Act and GDPR, using mechanisms like Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions where applicable.

5.4 Children’s Privacy

The Site is intended for users aged 13 and older, in line with Roblox’s requirements and the Data Privacy Act. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us to request its deletion.

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or technology. The “Effective Date” at the top will indicate the latest version. Significant changes will be communicated via a website banner, email (if provided), or other reasonable means.
